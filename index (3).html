Science-backed tips to help you build habits that actually stick.

⚡
Start tiny
Make habits so small they feel embarrassing. 2 push-ups. 1 page. 1 minute. Starting is everything — size comes later.
📎
Habit stacking
Attach a new habit to one you already do. "After I pour my morning coffee, I will meditate for 1 minute."
🌅
Morning wins
Willpower is highest in the morning. Schedule your hardest habits before noon to maximize your success rate.
🔗
Never miss twice
Missing once is an accident. Missing twice is the start of a new bad habit. One day off is fine — never miss two in a row.
🏆
Reward yourself
Right after completing a habit, do something enjoyable. This trains your brain to crave the habit loop automatically.
